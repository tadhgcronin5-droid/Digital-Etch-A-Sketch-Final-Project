Shake Test Notes

The MPU6500 IMU was tested by shaking the project while the OLED displayed a drawing.
The double-shake detection feature cleared the display when two shakes occurred within the two-second time window.

The minShakeGap value of 300 ms helped prevent one physical shake from being counted multiple times.
The shakeThreshold value of 1.5 was used to avoid accidental clears during normal handling.
